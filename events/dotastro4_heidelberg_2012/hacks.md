# Hacks from .Astronomy 4

.Astronomy 4 was held at the Haus der Astronomie and Internationales Wissenschaftsforum Heidelberg, 9–11 July 2012.

## Hacks

### AstroScanr
**Creators:** Rob Simpson, Sarah Kendrew, Karen Masters  
A visualisation of astronomy journal titles and abstracts using natural language processing techniques.  
[More info](https://orbitingfrog.com/2012/08/04/authorship-in-astronomy/)

### Astroworld
**Creator:** Carolina Odman-Govender  
Collect information about astronomical facilities around the world, including old, abandoned observatories, flashy new ones, future ones, and archeo-astronomical sites, using EpiCollect.  
[More info](http://dotastronomy.com/blog/2012/08/astroworld-mapping-astronomical-facilities-worldwide/)

### Cosmology Calculator OS X Widget
**Creators:** Brooke Simmons, Stuart Lowe, Kevin Schawinski, Julie Steele  
A port of Ned Wright's Cosmology Calculator to an OS X widget.  
[Download](https://s3-eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/dotastro/cosmocalc.zip)

### Flora
**Creators:** Amit Kapadia, Phil Marshall, Laura Whyte  
An attempt to enable quantitative measurements on images in a browser, measuring the position, size, and brightness of objects in astronomy images. Uses the astrojs library for FITS images and coordinate transformations.  
[Live](http://ubret.s3.amazonaws.com/dotastro4/index.html#/examine/m101)

### Science: It's Universal
**Creators:** Amanda Bauer, Nicole Gugliucci  
An outreach video about what science is and what it is like to be a scientist.  
[Watch](https://vimeo.com/45711303)

### Sh*t Scientists Say
**Creators:** Niall Deacon, Emily Rice, Kelle Cruz  
A collection of what astronomers love, hate, and repeat.  
[Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M-AzxQeTaw4)

### Zoonibot
**Creators:** Adrian Price-Whelan, Chris Beaumont, Gabe Perez-Giz, Chris Lintott, David Hogg, Meg Schwamb  
A "Planet Hunters butler" for the Zooniverse Talk platform, ready to automatically answer questions and provide detail when users request information.  
[Live](https://twitter.com/zoonibot/)
