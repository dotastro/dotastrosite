# .Astronomy 4: Heidelberg, 2012

**Date:** 9-11 July 2012
**Location:** Haus der Astronomie (HdA) and Internationales Wissenschaftsforum Heidelberg (IWH), Heidelberg, Germany
**Theme:** Visualisation, JavaScript/Python in the browser, crowdsourcing, career development, hacking the literature, global education
**Attendance:** ~60 people
**Organisers:** Sarah Kendrew, Thomas Robitaille (MPIA), Markus Possel (HdA)

## About

The fourth .Astronomy was hosted by the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy in Heidelberg, using two venues: the recently completed Haus der Astronomie (a centre for public engagement) and the Internationales Wissenschaftsforum in Heidelberg's historic centre.

Speakers included Prof Bruce Berriman (Caltech), Dr Tom Robitaille (MPIA), Dr Robert Simpson (Oxford), Dr Matthew Turk (Columbia), Dr Eli Bressert (Exeter/ESO), Geert Barentsen (Armagh Observatory), Dr Matthew Graham (Caltech), Prof David Hogg (NYU), Jonathan Fay (Microsoft Research), Dr Aleks Scholz (DIAS), Dr Robert Hollow (CSIRO), Julie Steele and Noah Iliinsky (O'Reilly Media), and Dr Arfon Smith (Adler Planetarium).

The conference was summarised in an "Unproceedings" paper on the arXiv (arXiv:1301.5193).

Supported by MPIA, the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG), and the Haus der Astronomie.
