The eighth .Astronomy conference was held at Pembroke College, Oxford. It was organised by Becky Smethurst, an Oxford PhD student at the time, supported by Chris Lintott and Sarah Kendrew. This edition also featured a Day 0 tutorials day, following the success of its introduction at .Astronomy 7 in Sydney.

## Programme

Talks covered open science in the Kepler missions, hardware hacking, citizen science, LSST data schools, the use of library archives in astronomy, and ESA Sky.

Talk contributors: Adam Miller, Carl Ferkinhoff, Ali Swanson, Geert Barentsen, James Gilbert, Tom Robitaille, Sarah Kendrew, and Daina Bouquin.

Pre-dinner talk: Aleks Scholz on WETI, the Wait for Extraterrestrial Intelligence.

Day 0 tutorials covered: various programming languages and tools, GitHub, web hosting, Flask, D3, webscraping, Django, and argparse. Tutorial contributors included Sarah Kendrew, Arna Karick, Demitri Muna, Scott Thomas, Lisa Ballard, Brian Cherinka, Phillip Plewa, Thomas Robitaille, Edward Gomez, and Geert Barentsen.

The Hack Day took place on Wednesday 22nd June 2016.
