# Hacks from .Astronomy 13

.Astronomy 13 was held at the European Space Astronomy Centre (ESAC), near Madrid, Spain, 23–26 April 2024.

Projects were pitched and developed during the hack day. The official hackathon template repository is [ESA-Datalabs/dotAstronomy13](https://github.com/ESA-Datalabs/dotAstronomy13).
