<div class="hack-title">Don't call me Colin</div>
<div class="hack-creators">Niall Deacon, Ruth Angus</div>
<p class="hack-desc">The International Astronomical Union has announced that extrasolar planets might get more interesting names. This is making one planet, Gliese 581d, a bit angsty. It really wants a nice mythical name like the Solar System planets and not to be named after celebrities or you mum or your cat (Colin).</p>
