The fifth .Astronomy conference was held in Cambridge, Massachusetts. It was the first .Astronomy event in North America, bringing the conference to a new continent and a new community of astronomers, educators, and technologists.

## Hacks

[3 hacks documented](hacks.md)

Hacks from this event include:
- **Don't Call Me Colin** (Niall Deacon, Ruth Angus): A video about a planet (Gliese 581d) that really wants a proper mythical name rather than being named after someone's cat.
- **D3PO** (Adrian Price-Whelan, Josh Peek, Chris Beaumont, Elisabeth Newton, Michelle Borkin): A project to allow astronomers to make interactive, publication-quality figures with linked brushing across plots. [Source](https://github.com/adrn/d3po) | [Live](http://www.d3po.org/)
- **Python client for the NASA ADS API** (Andy Casey): A Python module to interact with NASA's ADS that Doesn't Suck. [Source](https://github.com/andycasey/ads)
