# Hacks from .Astronomy 7

.Astronomy 7 was held at the Justice and Police Museum, Sydney, 3–6 November 2015.

## Hacks

### Life Cycle of Astronomers
**Creator:** Jennifer Piscionere  
Visualising astronomy career paths with D3.js.  
[Source](https://github.com/jpiscionere/jpiscionere.github.io) | [Live](http://jpiscionere.github.io/Sequences_sunburst.html)

### Friendly Virtual Radio Interferometer
**Creators:** Cormac Purcell, Nuria Lorente, George Howitt, Andrew O'Brien, Demitri Muna  
_Description to be added._

### Literal Virtual Observatory
**Creator:** Aidan Hotan  
Drive around telescope observatories in a Mad Max/gamer-style interactive visualisation.

### Pudding to the Max
**Creator:** Vanessa Moss  
Catch up with the exploits of the Cosmic Pudding at .Astronomy through a Koalas-to-the-Max style interface.  
[Source](http://github.com/rhobslein/tothemax) | [Live](http://t.co/QIj14OYPcK)

### Radio Yourself
**Creator:** Kirsten Gottschalk  
Upload an image and see what you'd look like through a radio telescope.  
[Live](http://kirstengottschalk.github.io/) | [Source](http://github.com/kirstengottschalk/kirstengottschalk.github.io)

### robo-ph
**Creators:** Tom Robitaille, Josh Peek, Katie Mack, Arna Karick  
A podcast that reads astro-ph abstracts one by one.  
[Source](https://github.com/astrofrog/robo-ph)

### Space Shark
**Creators:** James Gilbert, Simon Mutch, Dany Vohl, Pascal Elahi, Kieran Leschinski, Kirsten Gottschalk  
An alt-az pointer to be used for education.  
[Source](http://github.com/spaceshark)

### webcite
**Creators:** Becky Smethurst, Lisa Ballard, Demitri Muna  
Takes an ADS bibliographic code and plots a geographical web of citations for that paper. Finds the institutions of first authors of papers that cite the given paper.  
[Source](https://github.com/rjsmethurst/webcites_flask) | [Live](http://webcites.herokuapp.com)

### What's Up?
**Creator:** Aaron Robotham  
Updated Astroplan web app to help you plan observations.  
[Live](http://whatsup.icrar.org)
