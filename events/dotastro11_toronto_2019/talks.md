## Context

.Astronomy 11 brought the conference to Canada for the first time. Canada has a rich heritage in astronomy, including the development of Very Long Baseline Interferometry (VLBI), the discovery of Supernova 1987A, and observations of the first known black hole, Cygnus X-1. The event was hosted by the Dunlap Institute for Astronomy and Astrophysics at the University of Toronto.
