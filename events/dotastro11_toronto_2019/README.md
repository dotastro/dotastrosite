## About

The 11th .Astronomy was hosted by the Dunlap Institute for Astronomy & Astrophysics at the University of Toronto, bringing the conference to Canada for the first time.

Canada has a rich heritage in astronomy, including the development of Very Long Baseline Interferometry (VLBI), the discovery of Supernova 1987A, and observations of the first known black hole, Cygnus X-1. Toronto has a large community of astronomers with interests across both astronomy and software/data issues.
