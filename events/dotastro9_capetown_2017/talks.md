# Talks

<p class="page-lead">14-17 November 2017, Cape Town, South Africa</p>

## Themes

.Astronomy 9 explored how new technologies could help astronomical research, education, and outreach, with particular focus on:
- Machine learning and its applications in astronomy
- New web interfaces and software development
- Education techniques and research productivity
- Leveraging diversity for better science

## Context

The rapid growth of astronomy in South Africa, driven by projects like the Square Kilometre Array (SKA), the Southern African Large Telescope (SALT), and the IAU Office of Astronomy for Development, made Cape Town an exciting location for .Astronomy 9. This was the first .Astronomy held in Africa.
