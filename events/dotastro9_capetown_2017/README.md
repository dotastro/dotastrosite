# .Astronomy 9: Cape Town, 2017

**Date:** 14-17 November 2017
**Location:** South African Astronomical Observatory (SAAO), Cape Town, South Africa
**Theme:** Machine learning, web interfaces, software, diversity in astronomy
**Attendance:** ~60 people
**Organisers:** Hosted by the Office of Astronomy for Development (OAD) and SAAO

## About

The ninth .Astronomy conference took place at the South African Astronomical Observatory in Cape Town, hosted by the OAD and SAAO.

Set against the backdrop of South Africa's rapidly growing astronomy infrastructure, including the Square Kilometre Array (SKA), the Southern African Large Telescope (SALT), and the Office of Astronomy for Development, the conference explored advances in new technologies alongside a vibrant, growing community.

Conference website: https://dotastronomy9.saao.ac.za/
