## About

.Astronomy 12 was held at the Center for Computational Astrophysics (CCA) at the Flatiron Institute in New York. The event ran from Tuesday to Thursday, with an optional Day 0 tutorial day on Monday and continued hacking on Friday.

The event was held in person with live broadcast and recording of plenary talks.
