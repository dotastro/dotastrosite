.Astronomy 12 was held at the Center for Computational Astrophysics (CCA) at the Flatiron Institute in New York. The event ran from Tuesday to Thursday, with an optional Day 0 tutorial day on Monday and continued hacking on Friday. Plenary talks were live-broadcast and recorded.

.Astronomy is not a traditional conference. It is a gathering of around 100 astronomy enthusiasts, passionate about how the web and technology can transform science — an opportunity to expand your toolset for research, communication, and collaboration.
