# The .Astronomy Community

Since 2008, .Astronomy has built a vibrant community of over 300 alumni in astronomy, software development, education, and science communication.

## What Makes Us a Community

.Astronomy alumni stay connected long after the conferences end. The skills shared, projects started, and friendships formed at .Astronomy events continue to shape careers and collaborations across the field.

## Get Involved

- Attend a future .Astronomy event
- Contribute to this archive via [GitHub](https://github.com/dotastro/dotastrosite)
- Follow [@dotastronomy](https://twitter.com/dotastronomy) on Twitter

## Alumni

See the [alumni list](alumni.md) for a (growing) directory of .Astronomy participants.

## The .Astronomy Foundation

The [.Astronomy Foundation](https://www.dotastronomy.com/foundation) supports projects and collaborations that emerge from the conference series and the wider community.
