# .Astronomy: dotastronomy.com

The official archive and website for the .Astronomy conference series, running since 2008.

## About

The .Astronomy conference series builds a dynamic and creative community of scientists and educators to exploit the potential offered by modern computing and the internet in the era of data-driven astronomy. Through talks, tutorials, unconferences and hack days, participants gain new skills and broaden their views on what a career in astronomy can look like.

Since 2008, .Astronomy has created a vibrant community of over 300 alumni in astronomy and related fields.
