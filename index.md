# .Astronomy

**Building community at the intersection of astronomy and the modern web.**

The .Astronomy conference series brings together astronomers, developers, educators, and communicators to explore how technology can transform our science. Since 2008, we have built a vibrant community of over 300 alumni passionate about leveraging technology for the advancement of astronomy.
