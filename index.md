## About .Astronomy

Since 2008, .Astronomy has convened at universities, planetariums, observatories, and online to hack on projects at the intersection of astronomy and the modern web. Across 14 events on 5 continents, we have built tools, tutorials, sonifications, visualisations, games, and community.
